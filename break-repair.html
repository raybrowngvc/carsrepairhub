Our Brake Repair and Service Offerings

1. Brake Inspection and Diagnosis

  • Comprehensive Brake Inspection: Our technicians perform a thorough inspection of your entire braking system, including brake pads, rotors, calipers, and brake lines.
  • Diagnostic Testing: Using advanced diagnostic equipment, we can identify issues such as uneven wear, fluid leaks, and electronic malfunctions in your brake system.

2. Brake Pad and Shoe Replacement

  • Brake Pad Replacement: Worn-out brake pads can reduce braking efficiency and increase stopping distances. We replace brake pads with high-quality components tailored to your vehicleâ€™s specifications.
  • Brake Shoe Replacement: For vehicles with drum brakes, we offer brake shoe replacements to ensure effective braking performance.

3. Brake Rotor and Drum Service

  • Rotor Resurfacing and Replacement: Warped or damaged rotors can cause vibrations and reduced braking performance. We offer rotor resurfacing and replacement services to restore smooth braking.
  • Drum Resurfacing and Replacement: Similar to rotors, brake drums can wear out over time. Our technicians can resurface or replace brake drums to maintain optimal braking.

4. Brake Fluid Service

  • Brake Fluid Replacement: Old or contaminated brake fluid can lead to reduced braking performance and potential system damage. We flush and replace the brake fluid to ensure your braking system operates efficiently.
  • Brake Line Inspection and Repair: We inspect brake lines for leaks, cracks, or corrosion and provide necessary repairs or replacements to maintain fluid integrity and pressure.

5. Brake Caliper Service

  • Caliper Inspection and Repair: Malfunctioning calipers can cause uneven braking and increased wear on brake pads and rotors. We inspect and repair or replace faulty calipers to ensure even and effective braking.
  • Caliper Cleaning and Lubrication: Regular cleaning and lubrication of calipers can prevent sticking and ensure smooth operation.

6. Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) Service

  • ABS Diagnostics: We use specialized diagnostic tools to identify and resolve issues with your ABS, ensuring it functions correctly in emergency braking situations.
  • ABS Repair and Maintenance: From sensor replacements to module repairs, we offer comprehensive ABS services to keep your system in top condition.

Signs Your Brakes Need Service

Recognizing the signs of brake issues early can prevent more significant problems and ensure your safety. Look out for the following warning signs:

  • Squeaking or Squealing Noises: High-pitched noises when braking can indicate worn brake pads.
  • Grinding Sounds: A grinding noise may mean the brake pads are completely worn out, and metal components are rubbing together.
  • Vibrations: Feeling vibrations or pulsations in the brake pedal or steering wheel can signal warped rotors.
  • Soft or Spongy Brake Pedal: A brake pedal that feels soft or spongy when pressed could indicate air in the brake lines or a brake fluid leak.
  • Longer Stopping Distances: If your vehicle takes longer to stop than usual, itâ€™s time to have your brakes inspected.
  • Brake Warning Light: A brake warning light on your dashboard indicates a problem that needs immediate attention.
