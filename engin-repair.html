Our Engine Service and Repair Offerings

1. Engine Diagnostics

  • Comprehensive Engine Inspection: We perform a thorough inspection of your engine to identify any signs of wear, leaks, or damage.
  • Advanced Diagnostics: Using state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment, we can pinpoint issues with your engine’s performance, including trouble codes, misfires, and sensor malfunctions.

2. Routine Engine Maintenance

  • Oil and Filter Changes: Regular oil changes are essential for maintaining engine health. We use high-quality oil and filters to keep your engine lubricated and running smoothly.
  • Spark Plug Replacement: Worn spark plugs can lead to poor fuel efficiency and performance issues. We replace spark plugs to ensure optimal combustion.
  • Timing Belt/Chain Replacement: A failing timing belt or chain can cause significant engine damage. We offer replacement services to prevent engine failure.

3. Cooling System Services

  • Radiator Repair and Replacement: We inspect and repair or replace faulty radiators to prevent overheating and ensure efficient engine cooling.
  • Coolant Flush: A coolant flush removes old coolant and contaminants, helping to maintain your engine’s temperature and prevent corrosion.
  • Water Pump Service: We check and replace water pumps to ensure proper coolant circulation.

4. Fuel System Services

  • Fuel Injector Cleaning: Clean fuel injectors improve fuel efficiency and engine performance. We offer professional cleaning services to remove deposits and ensure proper fuel flow.
  • Fuel Pump Replacement: A failing fuel pump can cause engine stalling and poor performance. We provide replacement services to keep your fuel system functioning correctly.
  • Throttle Body Service: We clean and service the throttle body to maintain smooth acceleration and idle.

5. Engine Repair and Rebuild

  • Component Repairs: We repair or replace faulty engine components, including gaskets, seals, belts, and hoses, to prevent leaks and ensure proper engine operation.
  • Engine Rebuilds: For engines with extensive wear or damage, we offer comprehensive rebuild services, replacing worn parts and restoring your engine to like-new condition.
  • Head Gasket Repair: A blown head gasket can cause severe engine issues. We offer repair services to fix leaks and prevent further damage.

6. Performance Upgrades

  • High-Performance Parts: Upgrade your engine with high-performance parts, including camshafts, pistons, and intake systems, for improved power and efficiency.
  • Custom Tuning: Our experts can custom-tune your engine for optimized performance, enhancing horsepower, torque, and overall drivability.

Signs Your Engine Needs Service

Early detection of engine problems can prevent costly repairs and extend the life of your vehicle. Look out for the following warning signs:

  • Check Engine Light: An illuminated check engine light indicates a problem that needs immediate attention.
  • Unusual Noises: Knocking, pinging, or rattling sounds from the engine can signal serious issues.
  • Poor Performance: Reduced power, sluggish acceleration, or difficulty starting the engine.
  • Excessive Exhaust Smoke: Blue, black, or white smoke from the exhaust can indicate engine problems.
  • Overheating: Frequent overheating may be a sign of cooling system issues or engine damage.
  • Fluid Leaks: Puddles of oil, coolant, or other fluids under your vehicle.
