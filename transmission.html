Your vehicle's transmission is a crucial component that ensures smooth gear changes and efficient power delivery. Our Transmission Services

1. Transmission Inspection and Diagnosis

  • Comprehensive Inspection: Our technicians will perform a thorough inspection of your transmission system, checking for any signs of wear, leaks, or damage.
  • Diagnostic Testing: We use advanced diagnostic tools to accurately identify any issues with your transmission, including electronic control problems and mechanical failures.

2. Transmission Fluid Services

  • Fluid Replacement: Regular transmission fluid changes are essential for maintaining optimal performance and preventing overheating and wear. We use high-quality transmission fluids that meet your vehicle manufacturer’s specifications.
  • Fluid Flush: A complete transmission fluid flush ensures that all old fluid is removed and replaced with fresh fluid, helping to extend the life of your transmission.

3. Transmission Repairs

  • Leak Repairs: Our team can identify and repair transmission fluid leaks, preventing further damage and ensuring your transmission operates efficiently.
  • Component Repairs: We handle repairs to key transmission components such as solenoids, sensors, and valves, ensuring precise and smooth gear shifts.
  • Clutch Repairs: For manual transmissions, we offer clutch repairs and replacements to address issues like slipping or difficulty shifting.

4. Transmission Rebuilds and Replacements

  • Transmission Rebuilds: When extensive damage occurs, a transmission rebuild may be necessary. Our technicians will disassemble, clean, and replace worn or damaged parts, restoring your transmission to like-new condition.
  • Transmission Replacements: If a rebuild is not feasible, we offer complete transmission replacements with high-quality, factory-rebuilt or new units.

5. Performance Upgrades

  • High-Performance Transmissions: For enthusiasts seeking improved performance, we offer transmission upgrades tailored to your driving needs, including high-performance clutches, torque converters, and shift kits.
  • Custom Tuning: Our experts can custom-tune your transmission for optimized performance, enhancing acceleration, shifting speed, and overall drivability.

Signs Your Transmission Needs Service

It’s important to recognize the signs of transmission problems early to avoid costly repairs. Look out for the following warning signs:

  • Strange Noises: Grinding, whining, or clunking sounds when shifting gears.
  • Slipping Gears: The transmission unexpectedly slips out of gear or has trouble staying in gear.
  • Delayed Shifting: Hesitation or delay when shifting between gears.
  • Fluid Leaks: Puddles of red or brown fluid under your vehicle.
  • Burning Smell: A burning odor, which may indicate overheating transmission fluid.
  • Warning Lights: Transmission warning lights or check engine lights on your dashboard.
